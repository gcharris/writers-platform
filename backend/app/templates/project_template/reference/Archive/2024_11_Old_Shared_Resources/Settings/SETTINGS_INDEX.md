## Relationship Between Settings

### Connected Locations
- **Las Vegas Strip** connects to **The Venetian** via main thoroughfare
- **Outdoor Mall** connects to **Las Vegas Dojo** via pedestrian walkway
- **Mini Amusement Park Playground** connects to **Las Vegas Dojo** via short walk

### Nested Locations
- **The Venetian** contains multiple casinos, restaurants, and shops
- **Las Vegas Dojo** contains training areas and administrative offices
