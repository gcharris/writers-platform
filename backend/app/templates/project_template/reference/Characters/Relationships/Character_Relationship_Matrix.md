  - **Outcome:** Mickey and Noni become Ken's "eyes and ears" in the quantum space, operating under CIA oversight.
- **& Dr. Richard Evans:**
  - **Initial:** Richard sees Mickey as a research subject for his utopian vision of consciousness elevation. Mickey is desperate for a "cure" for his addictions.

- **& Dr. Dee (Dünkelspühler):**

  - **Initial:** Ben, an operative for Julian Vance, is initially a mysterious figure who observes Mickey.
  - **Alliance & Revelation:** Ben, disillusioned with Vance's "vision," provides critical warnings and information to Mickey about Dee's soul-harvesting and the true nature of the Q5 implants, forming a covert alliance.
  - **Shared Mission:** They work together, with Ben leveraging his technical and tactical skills, to try and disrupt Dee's plans and escape the facility.
  - **Continuing Ambiguity:** Despite their alliance, Ben's loyalty remains complex, as he still works for Vance, highlighting the difficult choices made in their world.
- **& Shifu Zhao:**
  - **Past Mentorship:** Zhao was Mickey's martial arts teacher, who observed Mickey's exceptional external talent but noted his "wild spirit".
  - **Seeking Blessing:** Mickey returns to Zhao, seeking his "blessing" for the experimental program, employing a mix of genuine vulnerability and strategic manipulation. Zhao, despite his skepticism of shortcuts, grants it, recognizing Mickey's deeply ingrained determination.
  - **Mutual Respect:** Zhao later expresses pride and gratitude for Mickey and Noni's teaching, acknowledging that Mickey's spirit was not "tamed" but rather directed.
- **& Sadie:**
  - **Brief Encounter:** Sadie is a young runaway who briefly connects with Mickey, sharing her naive dreams about Julian Vance's "makers commune".
  - **Mickey's Warning:** Mickey, recognizing the danger from his own experiences, attempts to warn her about Vance's technology being a form of control, seeing it as "leashes" not liberation.
- **& Cheri:**
  - **Transactional:** Their relationship is primarily transactional and exploitative, based on mutual use for sex, drugs, and money.
