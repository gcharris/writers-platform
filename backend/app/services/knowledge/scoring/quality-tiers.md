# Quality Tiers for Scene Scoring

## Overview

Explants scenes are scored 0-100 and assigned quality tiers based on total score.

## Tier Definitions

### Gold Standard (95-100 points)
**Description:** Publication-ready professional work that exemplifies Explants craft standards.

**Characteristics:**
- Zero anti-pattern violations
- Perfect voice consistency (cognitive fusion maintained throughout)
- Metaphor discipline (domains consistent, all comparisons earned)
- Character consistency flawless
- Phase-appropriate complexity

**What it means:**
- Ready for publication without revision
- Can serve as teaching example
- Demonstrates mastery of all craft elements

---

### A+ Tier (90-94 points)
**Description:** Excellent work with minor polish needed.

**Characteristics:**
- Maximum 1-2 formulaic anti-patterns
- Strong voice consistency (1-2 minor slips)
- Metaphor discipline solid
- Character consistency strong

**What it means:**
- One quick polish pass and ready
- Professional quality
- Minor fixes only

---

### A Tier (85-89 points)
**Description:** Strong professional work needing targeted revision.

**Characteristics:**
- Few formulaic anti-patterns (3-5)
- Good voice consistency with occasional lapses
- Metaphor mostly disciplined
- Character mostly consistent

**What it means:**
- Needs focused revision on specific issues
- Strong foundation
- Clear path to A+ with targeted fixes

---

### B+ Tier (80-84 points)
**Description:** Competent work needing moderate revision.

**Characteristics:**
- Some formulaic anti-patterns (5-8)
- Voice consistency needs work
- Metaphor discipline inconsistent
- Character has minor issues

**What it means:**
- Needs another draft pass
- Competent but not polished
- Multiple areas need attention

---

### B Tier (75-79 points)
**Description:** Adequate work needing significant revision.

**Characteristics:**
- Formulaic patterns present throughout
- Voice consistency weak
- Metaphor undisciplined
- Character may have issues

**What it means:**
- Needs substantial revision
- Basic competence shown
- Multiple craft elements need work

---

### C Tier (60-74 points)
**Description:** Needs significant work on craft fundamentals.

**Characteristics:**
- Anti-pattern violations present
- Voice consistency poor
- Metaphor random or absent
- Character consistency questionable

**What it means:**
- Major revision needed
- May need restructuring
- Fundamental craft issues

---

### D Tier (40-59 points)
**Description:** Fundamental craft issues throughout.

**Characteristics:**
- Zero-tolerance anti-patterns may be present
- Voice inconsistent or observer-dominant
- No metaphor discipline
- Character issues significant

**What it means:**
- Needs complete rewrite
- Craft fundamentals not mastered
- Extensive learning needed

---

### F Tier (0-39 points)
**Description:** Does not meet basic Explants standards.

**Characteristics:**
- Multiple zero-tolerance violations
- Voice entirely observer-based
- No craft discipline visible
- Character inconsistent or missing

**What it means:**
- Start over
- Review craft standards
- May not understand Explants principles

## Score Calculation

Total score is sum of:
- **Voice Authenticity:** 30 points max
- **Character Consistency:** 20 points max
- **Metaphor Discipline:** 20 points max
- **Anti-Pattern Compliance:** 15 points max
- **Phase Appropriateness:** 15 points max

**Total:** 100 points possible

## Using Tiers

**For Writers:**
- Aim for A-tier minimum before showing work
- A+ is publishable with light polish
- Gold is your target for final work

**For Editors:**
- B+ or lower: Send back for revision
- A-tier: Work with writer on targeted fixes
- A+: Light polish only
- Gold: Accept as-is

**For Learning:**
- C/D tier = learning fundamentals
- B tier = developing competence
- A tier = approaching professional
- A+/Gold = professional mastery
